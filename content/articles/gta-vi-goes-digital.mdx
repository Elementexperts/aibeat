This move by Rockstar Games marks a significant shift towards digital-only releases, leaving many to wonder about the future of physical games.</p><h3>What Happened?</h3><p>The news of GTA VI's digital-only release was met with a mix of reactions from fans and developers alike. While some see this as a natural progression of the gaming industry, others are concerned about the implications of abandoning physical discs. The decision to go digital-only is likely driven by the increasing popularity of online gaming platforms and the desire to reduce production and distribution costs.</p><h3>Why It Matters</h3><p>The shift towards digital-only releases has significant implications for game developers, founders, and the industry as a whole.
