<h2>Introduction to AI-Generated Music</h2> 
<p>Music streamer Deezer has announced that more than 50% of daily uploads on its platform are AI-generated, with over 90,000 tracks being uploaded daily in June. This staggering statistic has significant implications for the music streaming industry and content creation as a whole.</p> 
<h3>Why It Matters</h3> 
<p>The rise of AI-generated music poses both opportunities and challenges for developers, founders, and the music industry at large. On one hand, AI-generated music can increase music production efficiency and reduce costs. On the other hand, it raises concerns about authorship, ownership, and the potential for AI-generated music to displace human creators.</p> 
<h2>Implications for Developers and Founders</h2> 
<p>Developers and founders must take notice of this trend and consider its impact on their businesses. For developers, this means <strong>integrating AI-generated music detection capabilities</strong> into their music streaming platforms to ensure transparency and compliance with copyright laws. Founders, on the other hand, must <strong>rethink their content creation strategies</strong> and consider the role of AI-generated music in their business models.</p>
